Welcome to Sour Tutorial!

This tour covers the fundamentals of the Sour scripting language and can be completed entirely within your browser. There is no installation required.

This page will guide you through the fundamental concepts of the Sour programming language. By the end of this tutorial, you'll be able to write simple Sour programs.

Each chapter in this tour contains:

  • Theory to introduce key concepts of the language with examples.
  • Practice with exercises to test your understanding of what you have learned.
  • Solutions for your reference.

In this tour you will learn about:

  • Variables
